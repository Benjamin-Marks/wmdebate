Executive Board

President: Ben Marks. Ben is a junior at William and Mary double majoring in Finance and Computer Science. Originally from Harrisonburg Virginia, he has been involved in debate since high school, where he competed in Lincoln-Douglas. Before serving as President, he was the team's VP of Operations. Around campus, Ben is a member of the Flat Hat online staff, a brother in the Alpha Kappa Psi Business Fraternity, and works in the Arts & Sciences IT Department.

Vice President of Operations: Jerusalem Demsas. Jerusalem is a sophomore at the college. She is originally from Potomac, Maryland and is double majoring in Economics and Government. Beyond debate, Jerusalem is also the Undersecretary of Voter Registration and involved in WCWM.

Vice President of Education: Brad Ewing.Brad is a junior at the college, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Physics. Originally from Santa Clarita, California, he is an avid burritophile, beach lover, and neon color enthusiast. At the College, he is involved in computational research in the optics laboratory in the Physics Department and enjoys hiking the trails around Matoka.

Vice President of Novice Mentorship and Development: India Braver. Originally from New Jersey, India Braver is now a junior at the College of William & Mary majoring in Government and Economics. When not debating, she is involved in International Relations Club, research, Alma Mater Productions, Humans of William and Mary, and a service fraternity. She is also really good at watching television and likes playing with puppies.

Vice President of Campus Affairs: Elise Orlick. Elise is a senior at the College, majoring in Government and minoring in French and Francophone Studies. She is from Springfield, Virginia, and competed in Public Forum debate during high school. She now oversees the team's on-campus events, including tournaments and visiting speakers. Outside of debate, Elise is also involved with W&M Young Democrats, Virginia21, political science research, and supporting Netflix's increasing prominence within the entertainment industry.
